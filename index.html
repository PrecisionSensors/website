Precision Sensors
Building radars for different applications is our passion. Using a variety of front end
antenna designs sourced from top end German companies and combined with our in
house signal processing capabilities, we deliver tailor made state of the art solutions
for our customers. The signal processing board comprises of STM32H7 Arm-Cortex-
M7 series micro controller which provides powerful DSP functionality and reliability
through which our clients can enjoy reliable stress free operation for years to come.
Currently we have the following products:
River Surface Velocity Radar
Traffic Speed Radar
Level Radar
Tidal and Sea Wave Height Measuring Radar
